Chrysoprase

Green

Chrysoprase have an apple green to deep green colour intensity which makes them resemble emeralds. However the cause of green in chrysoprase is nickel mineral while in emeralds is the chrome mineral.

Chrysoprase are wide spread around the world covering many continents and countries such as; Indonesia in Asia, Queensland and Western Australia on the Australian continent, Haneti and Tanzania in Africa, Germany and Poland in Europe, transcontinental countries of Russia and Arizona and California and Brazil on the American continent.

Chrysoprase are considered to have healing powers and invoke interest and affection for others, particularly between spouses or lovers. They are also thought to promote success and overcoming feelings of negativity such as nervousness, worry and unease.
